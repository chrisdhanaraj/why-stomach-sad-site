# Why Stomach Sad — Privacy Policy

*Last updated: March 22, 2026*

## The short version

Why Stomach Sad does not collect, store, or share any of your data outside your device. Everything you log stays on your phone.

## What data the app stores

The app stores the following on your device only:

- **Meal logs** — what you ate, the food tags you picked, and any notes you added
- **Stomach ratings** — how your stomach felt after each meal
- **Custom tags** — any food tags you created

This data lives in a local database on your iPhone. It is not uploaded, synced, or backed up to any server.

## What the app does not do

- Does not require an account or sign-up
- Does not collect your name, email, or any personal information
- Does not use analytics or tracking
- Does not send data over the internet
- Does not use cookies
- Does not share data with third parties
- Does not show ads

## Notifications

The app sends local notifications to remind you to rate your stomach after a meal. These notifications are scheduled on your device. They do not go through any external server.

You can turn off notifications at any time in your iPhone's Settings.

## Data deletion

To delete all your data, delete the app. Since nothing is stored outside your device, deleting the app removes everything.

## Children's privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app has no accounts and collects no personal information from anyone.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new date.

## Contact

If you have questions about this policy, open an issue on [GitHub](https://github.com/chrisdhanaraj/why-stomach-sad-site/issues).
